About Nigel Schouteren

Nigel Schouteren is a talented artist and sound engineer based in the Netherlands. With a strong passion for both art and music, Nigel has developed a unique skill set that allows him to excel in various creative fields. As a skilled sound engineer, he works with videographers to bring their projects to life, and as an artist, he constantly explores new mediums and techniques.

In addition to his work in art and sound engineering, Nigel is also an aspiring musician, continuously honing his craft and expanding his musical horizons. As part of his journey, Nigel is in the process of building a freelance company that will enable him to share his talents with a wider audience and grow alongside his music career.
