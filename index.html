About ENNES

ENNES is a talented artist and sound engineer based in the Netherlands. With a strong passion for both art and music, ENNES has developed a unique skill set that allows them to excel in various creative fields. As a skilled sound engineer, they work with videographers to bring their projects to life, and as an artist, they constantly explore new mediums and techniques.

In addition to their work in art and sound engineering, ENNES is also an aspiring musician, continuously honing their craft and expanding their musical horizons. As part of their journey, ENNES is in the process of building a freelance company that will enable them to share their talents with a wider audience and grow alongside their music career.
